## Overview

**STEAM-C3T** is a two-year Erasmus+ partnership project dedicated to strengthening **STEAM education** (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) through interdisciplinary learning and digital creativity. The consortium brings together universities, schools, and NGOs from Türkiye, Estonia, Poland, Belgium, and North Macedonia to co-design and pilot innovative teaching materials, toolkits, and digital learning modules.

The project’s mission is to connect **campus-level research and classroom practice**, empowering teachers and students to apply modern technologies and creative problem-solving to real-world sustainability and innovation challenges.

---

## Key Objectives

- Develop and implement a **STEAM Teacher Training Toolkit** supporting interdisciplinary pedagogical practices.
- Design and evaluate a **Digital Proficiency Kit** based on **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, enabling teachers and students to create interactive web-based artefacts.
- Foster **entrepreneurial and sustainable thinking** through the _Green STEAM Entrepreneurship Challenge_.
- Promote cross-institutional collaboration and knowledge exchange across Europe.

## Tallinn University’s Role

Tallinn University, through its **School of Digital Technologies**, leads the design and validation of the **Digital Proficiency Kit**.
Its responsibilities include:

- Developing open, browser-based learning modules in **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.
- Supporting teacher and student training in digital pedagogy.
- Coordinating the project’s final assessment and dissemination activities.

---

## Project Outputs

1. **STEAM Teacher Training Toolkit** – theoretical and practical modules for interdisciplinary teaching.
2. **Digital Proficiency Kit** – web-based coding and design learning environment (HTML, CSS, JS).
3. **Green STEAM Entrepreneurship Challenge** – competition promoting sustainable innovation among students.
4. **Final Report and Open-Access Resources** – publicly available on this GitHub organization under open licence.
